# Gnawfeast Clawpack

**Faction:** Skaven


## Battle Traits

### The Lurking Vermintide
*Once Per Battle Deployment Phase*  
Pick a friendly unit that has not been deployed to be the target. The target unit is set up in reserve in the tunnels below. Units in the tunnels below that have not used the 'Gnawhole Ambush' ability by the end of the third battle round are destroyed.


## Regiment Abilities *(pick one only)*

### Warpstone-Laced Bullets
*Once Per Battle Your Shooting Phase*  
That weapon has Crit (Mortal) this phase.

### Too Quick to Hit-Hit
*Passive*  
No mortal damage is inflicted on friendly units when they use Retreat abilities.


## Enhancements *(pick one only)*

### Lead the Seething Horde
*Reaction Call For Reinforcements*  
Instead of using the set-up instructions in the 'Call for Reinforcements' ability, the replacement unit can be set up wholly within 13" of this unit and not in combat.

### Skryre Connections
*Passive*  
Your general's Ratling Pistol has an Attacks characteristic of 2D6 instead of D6.

### Warpstone Charm
*Passive*  
Subtract 1 from save rolls for enemy units in combat with your general.

### Cloak of Stitched Victories
*Passive*  
Your general has Ward (5+).
