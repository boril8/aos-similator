# Spitewing Flight

**Faction:** Sylvaneth


## Battle Traits

### Song of the Hunt
*Passive*  
Gain 1 chord each time an enemy quarry is destroyed. 1 chord: +1 run/charge rolls for friendly units within 9" of quarry. 2 chords: +1 hit rolls for friendly units' attacks within 9" of quarry. 3+ chords: +1 wound rolls for friendly units' attacks within 9" of quarry.

### Target of Vengeance
*Start of Battle Round*  
If there is no enemy quarry on the battlefield, pick an enemy unit to be the quarry for the rest of the battle.

### Airborne Cohesion
*Passive*  
Friendly units have a coherency range of 2".


## Regiment Abilities *(pick one only)*

### Leaves on the Wind
*Reaction: Any Combat Phase*  
Pick a friendly unit in combat. It can immediately use the Retreat ability as if it were your movement phase and no mortal damage is inflicted on it.

### Lifebringers
*End of Any Turn*  
Heal (D3) each friendly unit.


## Enhancements *(pick one only)*

### Zephyrkin
*Passive (Reaction: Fight)*  
If your general charged this turn, they can move up to 2D6" after that Fight ability has been resolved. They cannot end that move in combat.

### Bold Spirit
*Once Per Battle, Any Hero Phase*  
For the rest of the turn, add 1 to wound rolls for friendly units' combat attacks while they are within your general's combat range.

### Cunning Pursuer
*Passive*  
While your general is in combat, add 1 to charge rolls for friendly units wholly within 12" of them. Add 2 instead while your general is in combat with the quarry.

### Head of the Hunt
*Passive*  
Add 1 to the Rend characteristic of your general's melee weapons while the enemy quarry is within 9" of and visible to them.
