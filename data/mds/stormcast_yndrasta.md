# Yndrasta's Spearhead

**Faction:** Stormcast Eternals


## Battle Traits

### Scions of the Storm
*Passive*  
Yndrasta and your Annihilators unit are not set up during the deployment phase. Instead, from the third battle round onwards, they can use Lightning-Strike Arrival: set up anywhere on the battlefield more than 6" from all enemy units.


## Regiment Abilities *(pick one only)*

### Drive Them Back
*End Of Any Turn*  
Pick any number of friendly units that are both contesting an objective and in combat. Each can make a pile-in move. For each unit that did so, roll a dice; on a 4+, inflict 1 mortal damage on an enemy unit within 1".

### Defend to the Last
*Passive*  
Friendly units have Ward (6+) while they are contesting an objective you control.


## Enhancements *(pick one only)*

### The Prime Huntress
*Passive*  
The Damage characteristic of Thengavar is 2D6 for attacks that target a Monster.

### Strike with the Tempest's Rage
*Passive*  
Your general has Strike-first if they charged in the same turn.

### Dazzling Radiance
*Once Per Battle Your Movement Phase*  
If your general was set up this phase, you can return 1 slain model to each friendly unit wholly within 12" of your general.

### Hawk of the Celestial Skies
*Once Per Battle Any Combat Phase*  
Until the end of the phase, add 1 to hit rolls for attacks made by friendly units while they are wholly within 12" of your general.
