# Bloodcrave Hunt

**Faction:** Soulblight Gravelords


## Battle Traits

### Death's Descent
*Passive*  
Your Vargheists unit is not set up during the deployment phase. Instead, from the third battle round onwards, it can use the following ability.

### The Hunger
*Passive*  
Each time a friendly Vampire unit uses a Fight ability, after all of its attacks have been resolved, Heal (X) that Vampire unit where X is the number of damage points allocated by those attacks.


## Regiment Abilities *(pick one only)*

### Endless Legions
*Once Per Battle Your Movement Phase*  
You can set up a replacement unit with D6+4 models anywhere on the battlefield more than 6" from all enemy units.

### Ruinous Chargers
*Any Charge Phase*  
Inflict D3 mortal damage on each enemy unit it passed across during that Charge ability.


## Enhancements *(pick one only)*

### Grave-Sand Shard
*Once Per Battle Reaction Skeleton Legion Within 9 Of General*  
Add 1 to each legion roll made for that unit.

### Cloud of Bats
*Once Per Battle Your Movement Phase*  
Remove your general from the battlefield and set them up again anywhere on the battlefield more than 6" from all enemy units.

### Aura of Night
*Passive*  
Ignore negative modifiers to save rolls for shooting attacks that target your general.

### Spirit Gale
*Your Hero Phase*  
On a 7+, inflict 1 mortal damage on each enemy unit on the battlefield.
