# Vigilant Brotherhood

**Faction:** Stormcast Eternals


## Battle Traits

### Shield of Azyr
*Once Per Battle Your Hero Phase*  
Until the start of your next turn, the target has Ward (5+).

### Storm Charge
*Once Per Battle Your Charge Phase*  
Pick a friendly unit that is not in combat. That unit can use Charge abilities this turn even if it used a Run ability this turn.


## Regiment Abilities *(pick one only)*

### Strike Where Needed
*Once Per Battle Reaction Retreat*  
Pick a friendly unit in combat to be the target. It can make a Retreat move. It does not take any mortal damage for doing so and can still use Charge abilities this turn.

### Blaze of Glory
*Once Per Battle Any Combat Phase*  
Each time a model in the target is slain, roll a dice. On a 4+, inflict 1 mortal damage on an enemy unit in combat with the target.


## Enhancements *(pick one only)*

### Hallowed Scrolls
*Passive*  
Your general has Ward (5+).

### Morrda's Talon
*Passive*  
Your general's Hallowed Greataxe has Crit (Mortal).

### Quicksilver Draught
*Once Per Battle Any Combat Phase*  
Your general has Strike-first this phase.

### Null Pendant
*Once Per Battle End Of Any Turn*  
Roll a dice for each enemy unit contesting the same objective as your general. For each roll, subtract that many from that unit's control score until the start of your next turn.
