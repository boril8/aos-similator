# Warpspark Clawpack

**Faction:** Skaven


## Battle Traits

### Always Three Clawsteps Ahead
*Once Per Phase Enemy Movement Phase*  
Pick a friendly unit that is not in combat. That unit can use the 'Normal Move' ability as if it were your movement phase.


## Regiment Abilities *(pick one only)*

### Warpstone-Laced Armour
*Once Per Battle Reaction Opponent Attacked Stormfiends*  
Your Stormfiends unit has Ward (4+) this phase.

### Endless Swarm of Rats
*Passive*  
When a friendly Clanrats unit uses its 'Seething Swarm' ability, you can return D6 slain models to that unit instead of D3.


## Enhancements *(pick one only)*

### Skilled Manipulator
*Passive*  
Your general has Ward (4+) while they are within 1" of any friendly Clanrats units.

### Skitterleap
*Your Hero Phase*  
On a 6+, remove your general from the battlefield and set them up again on the battlefield more than 6" from all enemy units. They cannot use Move abilities in the following movement phase.

### Cage of Warp Lightning
*Once Per Battle Any Combat Phase*  
On a 2+, the enemy unit has Strike-last this phase. On a 1, inflict 1 mortal damage on your general.

### Scurry Away
*Any Combat Phase*  
Roll a dice. On a 3+, this unit can immediately use the 'Retreat' ability as if it were your movement phase. If it does so, no mortal damage is inflicted on it.
